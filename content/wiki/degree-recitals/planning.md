In the semester prior to the degree recital, a folder will be created in the shared Recitals folder with your last name. Inside this folder will be two template files:

1. **Recital Prep template**
2. **Program template**

The Recital Prep template is an Excel database that contains two sheets:

1. **Recital Checklist and Deadlines:** This should be filled out and consulted on a regular basis as you make preparations for your recital.
2. **Recital Planner:** Once the recital date has been approved, this should be completed to help you create goals and deadlines for yourself as you prepare music. It is laid out similar to a curriculum level grid and will be used between you and your teacher to determine what will be performed in lessons.

Both of these files should be kept in the cloud-based shared folder (Microsoft Teams) in order for both the student and instructor to utilize and edit (the most current edits will always be synced in the cloud).
