order: 3

Recital requirements are somewhat flexible and customizable, but the following are suggested areas that can be covered in each recital:
### BM Junior
- Solo marimba
- Solo timpani
- Solo multi-percussion and/or solo snare drum
- Solo vibraphone
- Percussion duo
- Percussion chamber ensemble
### BM Senior and BME Senior
- Solo marimba
- Solo timpani
- Solo multi-percussion and/or solo snare drum
- Solo vibraphone
- Percussion duo
- Percussion chamber ensemble
- Concerto for feature soloist with ensemble
- Jazz vibraphone, with or without combo
- Jazz combo (drum set) with featured solos:
    - Trading four, eights, etc...
    - Solo over the form
    - Open solo
