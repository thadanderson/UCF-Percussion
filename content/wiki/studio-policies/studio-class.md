All percussion majors and minors are required to attend and participate in the weekly Percussion Studio Class as an integral aspect of applied percussion study. Classes will meet every Friday at 11:30 a.m. in room M150 or through a virtual video conference. Topical areas of presentation include: auxiliary percussion studies, drum set studies, percussion pedagogy, repertoire and listening studies, video reviews, class performances, jury previews, clinician development, instrument and mallet repair/construction, special projects, forum discussions, among other topics. We will often use this time for guest artist appearances as well.
